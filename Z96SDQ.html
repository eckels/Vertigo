Introducing Vertigo Diamond: Competitive Team

1/20/2016

By: Vertigo Studios

Introducing Vertigo's newest competitive team, Vertigo Diamond. Storming onto the fold season one under their former team, Synergy Quantum, this team has started to come into their own. Season two has been no different as they with two of the members at POA Gold. With StartingAllOver, Bubbagum1, and alexjchang filling the carry roles and Scogent affixed as support, this team looks to mesh their tightly knit play style to overcome enemies quickly and without hesitation.
