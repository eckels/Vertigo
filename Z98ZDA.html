Meet Vertigo Black

12/4/2015

By: Vertigo Studios

After a successful run in the VGL 2nd Qualifiers, Vertigo Black will now be looking ahead to a new challenge. The live NA Autumn Season Finals in Santa Ana, California. Vertigo Black was among one of the top 8 teams in NA to qualify for the tournament. Here is an introduction to each of the 5 members that will represent Vertigo Black in the upcoming tournament.

Vertigo Black in Santa Ana, California at the VGL Autumn Live Finals. (From left to right, Vikson, Chuck, Beowulf, RiiKzZ, and Josh)

Beowulf

Position: Support

Beowulf is the captain of Vertigo Black and has been a member for Vertigo longer then the rest of the team. He is a sophomore in high school and currently living in St. Louis. Beowulf, a long time Baseball player has always had the competitive spirit inside of him, and that has helped give him an edge throughout the tournament. As a big fan of mobile games, he started playing Vainglory in December of 2014, a month after the game's global release. He quickly established himself as a top tier player in Vertigo and achieved a preseason ranking of Vainglorious.

"I am super excited for the finals because our team finally gets to show what we are made of and it's the farthest we have gotten in a tournament before. I'm always looking at our mistakes and analyzing each match we play on what we can improve on. I'm looking forward to future tournaments that we can participate in."

Vikson

Position: Jungle

Vikson is currently 22 years old, born and raised in Northern California where he is also studying mechanical engineering at a state university. A former Warcraft and DOTA player, he began playing Vainglory about 9 months ago and achieved a preseason ranking of Vainglorious all while balancing family, work, school and Vainglory.

"Live finals are exciting for me, they are going to be my first competitive tournament in person. No strategies, I am just going to try my best."

RiiKzZ

Position: Lane and Jungle

RiiKzZ is one of the premier laners for Vertigo Black, as well as being a strong jungle carry. He stumbled upon Vainglory when it was featured on the App Store and has never stopped playing since. As a Ringo main, he also achieved a preseason ranking of Vainglorious.

"I am looking forward to meeting my team that I've been playing with for months and all of our competition that we've been playing against for a while. However, most importantly to have fun and do our best to place well in the finals."

BestChuckNa

Position: Lane and Jungle

Chuck is Vertigo Black's incredible Laner (with a few tricks up his sleeve). He is from Southern California where he does road service. Chuck is interested in pursuing Chemistry I the future, as it was his favorite subject in High School. Vainglory was the first moba he has ever played and was introduced to it by a friend, he was amazed by the gameplay and graphics and later reached the Vainglorious ranking in preseason.

"I am excited for the live finals because I am going to be able to meet the other exceptional teams and it's a great opportunity to compete."
