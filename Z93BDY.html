1 Year Anniversary - A Letter from our Leader

2/23/2016

By: JoshOnVertigo

Dear Community,

What a ride it has been! In the past year, Vertigo has done some amazing things and innovated the community in some astonishing ways. It has been a great pleasure to see how the Vainglory community has grown, and to witness the competitive eSports scene flourish.

When we first started Vertigo, we had the mindset of being the best community guild out there and to promote Vainglory to the masses. One year later, we are still striving toward that end while we endeavour to even greater accomplishments. Not only are we here to promote Vainglory to the best of our ability, but we also intend to field some of the best competitive teams in the Vainglory eSports scene.

We are proud to see that Vertigo Black is one of only eight teams that will advance to the live Vainglory Championship for the second time in a row. Our team is excited to compete and see all of their hard work pay off with a victory. Loyalty and mutual respect kept this team afloat through the harsh environment of the competitive scene. I also want to give a special shoutout to Super Evil Megacorp, especially to EdTheShred and PlayoffBeard. We had several conversations when Vertigo was first formed about my passion for Vainglory and the potential the game had in the future. They helped Vertigo transition to the organization it is today by promoting our guild, encouraging us, and much more. Another thanks goes to Vainshame for helping us with his several graphic designs including our scorpion logo and the jerseys that he helped us produce. I appreciate what he's done for us and I'm so happy that he's working over at SEMC now.

I end this letter by urging everyone to help Vainglory be the best mobile game with the best community. This game is already something great and it would be amazing if everyone would take part in working together on this roller coaster ride to continue to make VainGlory something amazing.

Yours truly,

JoshOnVertigo
